Kim Zogalis - Realtor



Kim has spent most of her life in North Attleborough, Massachusetts and truly feels this is home. She enjoys working with people and dedicates her spare time volunteering for various community organizations including Pop Warner and Keep North Attleborough Beautiful. She also loves looking at houses. Old, new, and in between. Every home has a story. Now that she is a Realtor she truly enjoys the process of helping people find the home of their dreams. Whether it is your very first home, and antique fixer upper, up sizing or downsizing. This process is a huge financial transaction, possibly the largest one you will ever make. It is important to her that her clients are well educated on the real estate they are investing their hard earned money into, and that they feel that a specific property is the right one for them. Selling a home can be time consuming and stressful and as a Realtor she can ease the process by guiding you through a smooth sale. Contact Kim, she looks forward to working with you to make selling and/or purchasing a home an exciting, easy and memorable process.
