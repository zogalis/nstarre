Introducing NorthStar

NorthStar Real Estate was founded in 2016 with a passion and commitment to offer a local, client driven success story. Comprised of experienced agents from different real estate backgrounds, we feel strongly about delivering a quality experience throughout the entire real estate transaction. Many of our agents are long time residents of the surrounding communities and offer first hand knowledge of Massachusetts. We maintain a strong network of contacts to assist in all areas of the sales process as well as throughout homeownership in the years beyond the closing. We are able to leverage that experience, and formulate sound advice for our clients as we strive to provide a seamless process, guiding you through a buying or selling transaction.
